About Me

I am a graduate of the University of Maryland, class of 1996. My major was computer science and my minor is in business. I started working at a software company in 1996 that specialized in payroll system software which linked time clocks and payroll systems. I eventually moved in an information systems financial consultant position and started my own consulting business.

Once I became a mother, I moved into a part-time role in the fitness industry. I am currently a cerified RRCA running coach and participate in several fitness programs. Although it is very rewarding, I am interested in going back into the software development industry.

At home, I have two teenagers. A boy, Daniel who is 17 and a girl, Gabrielle who is 15. Both of my kids are heavily involved in school and sports which keeps all of us very busy. We also have a Schnoodle named Molly. As a family, we enjoy traveling and have been to many destinations in Europe and the U.S.
